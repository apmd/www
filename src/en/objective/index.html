Summary

We create this Non-Profit Foundation to support the advance and prosperity of mindful modern life. The summary of our core objectives as follows:

  • Organizing inclusive social networks in an online economy
  • Implementing scalable systems with modern technology
  • Sustaining clean environment with reliable energy
  • Cooperating with other charitable organizations
  • Avoiding political conflict and division

Metrics

We are considering the following measurements to our progress, either within the frame of a small community or in an entire economic segment:

  • Immediacy and intimacy of engagement
  • Velocity of exchange
  • Efficiency

Inclusive networks in an online economy

Many segments of the current economy are scaled to supply the greatest number of people with decreasing capacity to respect and nurture our individual needs and preferences.

By our social objective we think a well connected network of ethical participants, where direct exchange and collaboration is effortless and accessible, creates a better social structure and economic experience.

Goals

  • Prepare proposals on opportunities for a better connected economy
  • Support the organization of inclusive online networks
  • Execute on the implementation and delivery of the proposed solutions

Metrics

  • Direct collaboration in a network
  • Experience of the individuals
  • Diversion between market participants
  • Scale of competition and variety

Scalable systems with modern technology

Technology enabled us to strengthen our security and focus a greater extent on our ethical well-being. However many parts of life are still lagging behind or have taken a direction where we end up with less optionality.

By our technical objective we can skillfully scale technology to modernize the underdeveloped segments of the economy and improve our individual freedoms.

Goals

  • Design and implement technical architectures for efficient and ethical networks
  • Develop open platforms and protocols to scale immediate collaboration
  • Sustain reliable function of online communities

Metrics

    Immediacy of desired economic exchange Efficiency and accessibility Cost of operation

Collective security from reliable energy

All systems have a certain inefficiencies and externalities and our reliance on unsustainable or unsafe energy sources can disintegrate our lives and our future.

By our ecological objective we want develop and integrate reliable applications of clean energy that we can understand and harness peacefully.

Goals

  • Analyze and propose sources and systems of reliable ethical energy
  • Support education and transition and maintenance to renewable systems
  • Introduce sustainable energy into the social networks

Metrics

  • Reliance on efficient on energy sources
  • Permanence and cleanliness
