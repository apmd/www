Problem

Family businesses are often short of technical expertise to engage with clients without intermediary agents, who actually have a significant impact on the experience and affordability of a service.

The promise of the internet revolution was the development of social networks that can function independently and efficiently allowing for greater freedom of information and engagement.

So far the technical advancement of the internet has increased access to various networks tremendously, however they exists inside centralized and industrialized systems exercising great influence on the quality and quantity information and engagement between market participants.

The challenge of this project is to continue the progress of the internet and develop the necessary freedom technology for people to personally and mindfully explore and exchange what they seek and offer.

Plan

The following prospective capabilities of hosts and guests define our vision and our long term plans. We are progressively refine these areas as we learn more from both research and development and align our priority accordingly.

Standardize

Categorizing services and adopting common contracts.

To create a suitable space for comparison and competition, hosts may assign any one of the open standards to the service they are offering.

Following similar standards across the market may help improve the quality of the service as the differences can become more visible and actionable.

With standardization the guests may understand better the type of service they are subscribing to and match their expectations and evaluation accordingly.

Car classification

Tokenize

Destructuring services to their basic fungible and marketable units.

To represent the essential value provided across time, hosts may break down their service into tradable units that can be exchanged and redeemed for their underlying offer.

The tokens bearing access to a service may be issued for the future so guests can submit or exchange reservations following market demand.

As the credibility of the tokens increase the hosts may create franchises and alliances or exclusively control the supply of their scarce assets.

Distribute

Recording the issuance and exchange of services verifiably.

To provide access to their services hosts may distribute vouchers to be redeemed according to their terms and standards.

Recording the distribution of the vouchers in a decentralized network can help to avoid double spending and also to verify or recover the state of past and outstanding offerings

Vouchers can become transferable between interested parties to find the highest bidder and to discover an optimal price for a service per supply and demand.

Integrate

Modernizing the utilization of services

To allow access for guests to order or redeem services independently the hosts may use digital applications and electronic instruments to automate the mechanical parts of their services.

Automating repetitive tasks can help to improve the overall service quality and reliability while also allowing more time to focus on personal and ethical exchange.

Technical optimizations can create a more competitive and efficient market for the benefit of all participants.

Search

Finding relevant and available services quickly.

To prepare a plan of activities guests may search the open dataset of services according to their standard criteria either in their home or on their journey abroad.

Looking through the lists of standardized services while seeking for a specific need or just exploring the available options is a key for enjoyable planning.

Having a wide variety of recommendations via diverse search platforms optimized for specific types of services can help finding and promoting the best value.

Order

Exchanging reservations on demand or in advance with flexibility.

To use a service either immediately or sometime in the future guests may arrange reservations directly with their preferred hosts or exchange access to services by offers from other guests.

The liability of canceling a reservation can become an asset for guests to transfer them freely to other parties who can make the best use of them.

Guests can organize and bid according to their preferences independently an open market without technical limitation by the hosts capacity to facilitate.

Access

Using the subscribed services immediately.

To access services in a standard and enjoyable manner guests may redeem their orders securely either offline or online without interruption.

As the hosts incrementally improve services their access can become as simple as guests scanning codes from screens or passes.

Even physical access to locations or assets may be simplified with standardization and automation.

Review

Providing constructive feedback safely.

To close the loop of improving services guests should be able to leave reviews safely and without risk of repercussion.

Developing a healthy feedback mechanism can help selecting for the highest value and also create genuine opportunity for hosts to improve.

Reviews can also sanitize stale or unreliable offerings from the network while keeping the market competitive.
