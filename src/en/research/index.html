Research

We engage in research of various subjects that are relevant to our objectives and the direction of their development.

Electronic authentication for mechanical locks

Creating a standard extension of electronic authentication for mechanical locks using open software and hardware protocols to advance the freedom of personal online leasing without counterparty risk.

Signed Unique Access Identifiers

Creating a URL and QR compatible standard of signed identifiers for decentralized authorization to real world personalized services

String representation of variable complexity date series

Creating a URL-friendly open standard to reconstruct variable complexity natural Date expressions using a minimal human-readable String syntax.
